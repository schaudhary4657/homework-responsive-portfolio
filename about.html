About Me

Sapana Chaudhary - Software Engineer & Web Developer

Sapana Chaudhary is a Software professional seeking for the career in the Information and Technology field. She is currently pursuing MS degree in Software Engineering at California State University Fullerton and will graduate in Spring 2020. She also holds MBA and BBA degree in Business Administration from Nepal. In short, she is a software professional with the business administration and management skills with over six years of working experience.

Prior to moving to California, USA from Nepal in early 2017, she spent working as an assistant manager in commodity market in Nepal where

she was responsible to maintain the compliance of the clients with the organization rules to conduct smooth operation of the business. She also worked very closely with the IT team to make sure the software platform (the main trading software) is customer friendly and easy to operate. Apart from this, she worked as an assistant manager in client service department in the same organization in which she was responsible to make sure all the client issues were handled with the right solutions at the right time.

Her hobbies include anything to do with software development and programming, movies, music, travel, swimming, and dancing.
